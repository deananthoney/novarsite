Compared with a CsA-based regimen, what was the renal function benefit achieved in the BENEFIT trial?


A. 4 to 6 mL/min higher mean mGFR at month 12


B. 8 to 10 mL/min higher mean mGFR at month 12


C. 13 to 15 mL/min higher mean mGFR at month 12

Correct.
The correct answer is:
C. 13 to 15 mL/min higher mean mGFR at month 12
At 12 months, renal function was superior in patients receiving belatacept versus CsA as demonstrated by all renal measures. In addition, the impact of having higher rates of acute rejection with belatacept did not negate its renal benefits.
